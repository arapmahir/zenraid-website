How to Play

Tactical water combat meets strategic team gameplay. Here's how the Raid begins.

01

Gear Up

Equip your electric blaster, strap on your smart vest, and sync your vibration wristband. Make sure everything is charged and connected. The battlefield awaits.

02

Choose Your Role

Draw from the tactical game card deck to select your class and objectives. Each role has unique abilities and win conditions—coordinate with your team for maximum strategy.

03

Battle to 50 Points

Score points through strategic hits, completing mission objectives, and coordinated teamwork. When you're hit, your vest lights up a red X and wristband vibrates—head to the respawn zone for a 30-second cooldown.

04

Claim Victory

First team to reach 50 points wins the Raid. Review stats, celebrate with your squad, and get ready for the next battle. The competition never stops.
