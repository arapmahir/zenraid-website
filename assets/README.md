## Image Specifications

### Product Images
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Size**: 600-800px width
- **Background**: Transparent or dark (#0A0A0F)

### OG Images
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Size**: 1200x630 pixels
- **Background**: Dark with ZenRaid branding

## Tips
- Use high-quality renders with consistent lighting
- Maintain consistent style across all product images
- Ensure transparent backgrounds for product shots
