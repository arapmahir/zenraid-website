Features

What Makes It Different

Not just water guns. A complete system for organized outdoor play.

Electric Power

No pumping needed. USB-rechargeable motors shoot water up to 18 meters with consistent power.

Automatic Hit Tracking

Vest sensors detect water instantly. LEDs show where you got hit, wristband vibrates to alert you.

Team Play

Split into teams, pick roles with game cards, and compete to reach 50 points first.

App Scoring

Mobile app shows live scores, player stats, team standings, and game history.

Safe for Everyone

Designed for ages 8+. Safety locks prevent accidents. All the fun without bruises or mess.

Looks Good

Modern design that doesn't look like a toy. Something you won't mind having around.
